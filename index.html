Jingyao Li（李靖瑶）

I am a Ph.D. candidate at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, advised by Prof. Jiaya Jia. I completed my undergraduate study at Xi'an Jiaotong University in the Special Class for the Gifted Young (少年班) and the Qian Xuesen Honor Class (钱学森班).

I am currently interested in foundation large language models, especially mid-training and agentic reinforcement learning.
